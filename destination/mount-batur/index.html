About Highlight Location Review

Mount Batur is an active volcano situated in Bali, Indonesia. Standing at about 1,717 meters above sea level, it's known for its mesmerizing sunrise trekking experiences. The volcano is surrounded by a beautiful caldera lake, adding to its scenic allure. Mount Batur's hiking trails are popular among adventurers and tourists looking to witness the breathtaking sunrise views over Bali. Despite its active status, it remains a frequented destination for its natural beauty and trekking opportunities.

  • With an elevation of approximately 1,717 meters above sea level, it's not the highest peak in Bali, but its accessibility and scenic beauty attract many climbers.
  • The sunrise trek up Mount Batur is a popular activity where hikers begin the ascent in the early hours to reach the summit before sunrise, offering breathtaking panoramic views.
  • The volcano is surrounded by a vast caldera with Lake Batur, providing stunning scenery and an additional element of beauty to the trek.
  • Mount Batur holds spiritual significance for the Balinese people, and there are temples around the base and along the trek route.
  • Apart from the sunrise, the summit offers panoramic views of Mount Agung, Mount Abang, and the surrounding landscape.
