About Highlight Location Review

Bangli is a regency located in the central part of Bali, Indonesia, known for its cultural heritage, lush landscapes, and traditional way of life. It's an area less frequented by tourists, offering an authentic Balinese experience. The regency encompasses Mount Batur, an active volcano, and the serene Lake Batur. Bangli is also home to the ancient village of Penglipuran known for its traditional architecture and customs. Visitors can explore historic temples like Pura Kehen and experience the region's rich culture, arts, and ceremonies in a tranquil and less crowded setting compared to other tourist-heavy areas of Bali.

  • Dominating the region's skyline, an active volcano offering hiking opportunities and stunning views.
  • Surrounds Mount Batur, providing a picturesque setting and opportunities for water-based activities.
  • An ancient village known for its well-preserved traditional Balinese architecture, customs, and cultural heritage.
  • A significant temple in Bangli, known for its historical importance and intricate Balinese architecture.
