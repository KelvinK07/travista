About Highlight Location Review

Bedugul is a scenic highland area located in the central part of Bali, Indonesia. Known for its cooler climate, stunning landscapes, and serene surroundings, it's a popular retreat for locals and tourists alike. Key attractions in Bedugul include the picturesque Lake Bratan with the iconic Ulun Danu Beratan Temple, a stunning water temple set against the backdrop of the lake and mountains. The area is also famous for its beautiful botanical gardens, traditional markets, and recreational activities like boating and strawberry picking. Additionally, Bedugul offers a peaceful escape from the coastal areas of Bali, providing a refreshing change of scenery with its lush greenery and cooler temperatures.

  • Central highland area in Bali, Indonesia, known for its cooler climate and stunning natural beauty.
  • Bedugul is home to Bali Botanic Garden, showcasing diverse flora, including orchids, medicinal plants, and a large collection of tropical plants.
  • Bedugul hosts bustling markets selling local produce, fruits, vegetables, and traditional crafts.
  • Offers a refreshing change from Bali's coastal heat, with cooler temperatures and a misty, serene atmosphere.
  • Features the iconic Ulun Danu Beratan Temple, a picturesque water temple set against the backdrop of the lake and surrounding mountains.
